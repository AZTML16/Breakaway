TUTORIAL
HUB each week: play games from the schedule or use SIM WEEK. In a game you get four timed moments; keys 1–4 pick an option. Timer empty means auto pick. Passing options skew assists, shooting skews goals.

League playoffs use a seeded bracket (field size varies). Your series is best-of-7 (first to four wins) — play games one at a time until the series ends. From the hub, SIM ROUND runs CPU series until it reaches yours (then stop); SIM MY SERIES fast-sims your matchup; PLAY GAME is the next live game. If you lose a series, SIM ROUND finishes the bracket and you go to the offseason. Playoff scoring is tighter than the regular season (especially for skill-first builds). Junior, college, and minor playoffs add extra squeeze on sim odds and on playable moments.

World tournaments: three round-robin group games, then single-game knockout rounds (no best-of series).

Career OVERALL PERFORMANCE blends every played moment score and every simmed game (from your box score for that sim). Both paths update the same grade.

CONTRACT tab: DEMAND TRADE after season 2 and 14 GP, once per season if not on cooldown. Works when your team sits low in the standings or has a weak win rate while your OVR or personal wins are strong. Outcome is random; NTC still controls whether you can refuse a deal.

New contracts are negotiated in the offseason when your current deal is expired. Lower right SETTINGS for music, SFX, and CRT options. Press ? or / on the hub for this screen.
REFERENCE
Create flow: nationality, gender, position, archetype, X-Factor, attributes, league at 16. Juniors lead to draft; college to scholarships. College and paid overseas semi-pro (NEHL/CEHL/ARHL, SDHL/FWHL/AWHL) are much easier from 17+; at 16 they only unlock if your preview OVR is elite (72+ men / 50+ women). Abroad, NEJC / CEJC / ARJC (men) and EWJC / AWJC (women) are the usual first step, then semi-pro offers — like NA juniors feeding NCHA. From year 2+ in junior, strong OVR may surface optional college or semi-pro; you can always stay in junior. Path can run junior or college, minors, then PHL or PWL. Social tab for teammates, staff, fans. Retire from the hub when you want a legacy summary.
